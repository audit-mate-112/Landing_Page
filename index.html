Let’s Address the Obvious

Real questions. Honest answers. No sales fluff.

"How is this different from bigger platforms?"
They optimize for continuous, year‑round monitoring, which usually means endpoint agents, nonstop alerts, and pretending you hired a full‑time CISO. We optimize for speed and snapshotting. Use them if you want a CISO‑in‑a‑box. Use AuditMate if you just need clean, defensible evidence to pass an audit.
"Do I need to install an agent?"
Absolutely not. We hate endpoint agents. They’re invasive, brittle, and everyone secretly disables them anyway. AuditMate connects via read‑only APIs to check configuration and access controls, not your employees’ browser history.
"What if our infrastructure is messy?"
Then congratulations — you’re a normal startup. Auditors don't expect perfection; they expect documentation. Our "Risk Acceptance" workflow lets you formally document known gaps with business justification. Messy infrastructure doesn’t fail audits. Undocumented messes do.
"Is my data secure?"
Yes. All stored credentials are encrypted using AES‑256 (Fernet). We never clone your repositories — we only read metadata. Evidence snapshots are SHA‑256 hashed, so you can prove nothing was altered after collection.
"Can I export the data?"
Yes. We’re not keeping it hostage. Export everything in PDF (executives), CSV (auditors), or Raw JSON (developers). If you leave AuditMate, you leave with all your proof. No lock‑in. No ransomware‑but‑make‑it‑SaaS.
"Can I invite my auditor?"
You can do better. Use Secure Courier to generate a time‑limited, read‑only link. Auditors see exactly what they need to verify compliance — nothing more, nothing less. No shared logins. No awkward Zoom screen‑sharing.

More Questions You’re Probably Thinking

"What frameworks do you support?"
SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other common frameworks startups are forced to care about. We map evidence once and reuse it across controls so you’re not answering the same question 14 different ways.
"How long does it take to get audit‑ready?"
For most teams: days, not months. You connect your systems, generate snapshots, document known risks, and export evidence. No prolonged compliance cosplay.
"Will this replace our auditor?"
No. Auditors are still very real people who send invoices. AuditMate replaces endless spreadsheets, screenshot archaeology, and the ritual panic before evidence deadlines. Your auditor will still audit. They’ll just do it faster.
"What happens after the audit?"
Nothing magical. Your company continues existing. You can keep AuditMate around for future audits, update snapshots when needed, or export everything and come back later. We don’t require eternal devotion.
"Is this overkill for an early‑stage startup?"
If you don’t need an audit, yes. Close the tab. If a customer, partner, or regulator is asking for compliance proof right now, then no — it’s exactly the right amount of kill.
"Who is this *not* for?"
Companies that want constant employee monitoring, teams seeking real‑time security alerting, or enterprises with a full GRC department. AuditMate is for teams who want to pass audits cleanly and move on with their lives.
